Z1X1

GOALS
-----
Worlds Adrift: Ships. Grappling Hooks.
H1Z1: Weapons. Movement.
SOSClassic: Voice Comms. Radios.
GTAV: Car Physics.
ARK: Primitive weapons (without latency...).

SYNOPSIS
--------
A co-op multiplayer third-person open-world aerial ship-based combat shooter.

FEATURES
--------
- Networking (Players, Voice, etc.)
- Physics (Object and Projectile)
- Player Models and Animations
- Weapon Models and Animations

OPTIONALS
---------
- Open Source
- Fully streamed development
- Intensely moddable
